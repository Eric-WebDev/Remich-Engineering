Welcome to Rimech Engineering

We’re Committed to quality

At Rimech Engineering, we're passionate about crafting bespoke metalworks that bring your vision to life. Our team of skilled welders and fabricators have years of experience creating everything from domestic railings to process pipework. We specialize in working with a variety of metals, including steel, stainless steel, copper nickel and aluminium, and can handle projects of all sizes and scopes. Whether you need a custom railing for your house, a structural steel fit out for your commercial building or industrial spec pipework, we have the expertise and tools to get the job done right. As a trusted name in the metal fabrication and pipe industry, we're committed to delivering superior quality and craftsmanship. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and vision, and always strive to exceed their expectations. We have industry experience in mining, oil and gas, pharmaceutical, marine, food and beverage along with domestic works. With our extensive experience and state-of-the-art equipment, we're able to deliver bespoke solutions that are tailored to your unique requirements. So, whether you're in North or south of the river in Perth, contact us today to learn more about how we can help bring your metal fabrication project to life.
